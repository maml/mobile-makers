1.3 - Why do you think it's important to be aware of the idiosyncracies with older versions of
Objective-C and to keep up with new features as they are added?

You may find code examples from an era of earlier versions and auto-generated code may have
appeared differently than in later versions so it's good to know what you're looking at.

As new versions are released things are often simplified so you'll want to take advantage of
time saving features.
